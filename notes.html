UDP-glucuronic acid decarboxylase 1 (UXS1) decarboxylates UDP-alpha-D-glucuronate (UDP-GlcA) to form UDP-alpha-D-xylose (UDP-Xyl). The reaction is localized to the Golgi apparatus (inferred from studies of the homologous rat enzyme (Moriarity et al. 2002).
